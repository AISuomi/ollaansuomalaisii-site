“Rakasta, kärsi ja unhoita” (Love, suffer and forget) is a trio tape by the Rantamäki sisters.

The accompaniment is performed by a studio orchestra. Fourteen songs are included, half of them composed by mother Martta.

The arrangements are by Reino “Rene” Karppinen from Kajaani. His song “Vanha mies” (The Old Man) is also found on the tape.
