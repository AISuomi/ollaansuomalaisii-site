Joululauluja (Christmas songs) is a collection of Martta’s own melodies. Lyrics are by Martta and also by Eila Pöntisenaho, Reetta Suo, Ulla Perälä and daughter Margit.

  • Lapsuusjoulu
  • Joululle

The songs are performed as a trio by Anna-Liisa, Margit and Merja with Martta & Otto on accordions.

A separate Christmas songbook “Joululauluja” is also available.
