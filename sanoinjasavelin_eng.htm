“Sanoin ja sävelin” (Words and melodies) I–III are songbooks and recordings with more than one hundred songs by Martta Rantamäki.

The songs speak about everyday life, Finnish nature and people. Many choirs and small bands have used the material in their own concerts.

The songbooks are available as:

  • Sanoin ja sävelin I
  • Sanoin ja sävelin II
  • Sanoin ja sävelin III

Songbooks and recordings can be ordered via the order form.
